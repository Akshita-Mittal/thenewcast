About

THE NEW CAST is a full service casting agency based in New York and London. Founded by British Casting Director, Fashion Film Director and Photographer Amy Rebecca Lowles, formally trained in Fine Art at The Cooper Union in NYC and The Slade School of Fine Art in London. The agency embodies her hands-on, customer-centric attitude and ethic. THE NEW CAST services a broad range of clients with a focus on fashion advertising, lifestyle, commercial, editorial and runway projects. We dig deep to understand the ethos of each brand we work with to discover an authentic, original aesthetic or transform an existing identity with a refined vision and a sensibility for what works.
